**Scope:** Incomplete implementations, conceptual gaps, user workflow logic gaps, cross-cutting concerns

---

## Executive Summary

The codebase is well-structured and internally consistent at the module level. The Phase 1.H–1.K implementations are solid and meet their AD goals. The primary risk concentration is in the **forge workflow**: phases 04 and 05 self-mark as successful when their work is absent, producing a "forge complete" outcome on a machine with no VMs and no k3s cluster.

A secondary risk is **dashboard assessment scores**: every deployed dashboard instance renders the "No readiness report found" placeholder because the score keys it reads (`ACS/RRS/DCS/CRS/OSS/PHS`) are never populated by any module in the codebase.
